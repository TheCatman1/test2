What is 3D animation?

3D-animation ‚Äî is the process of creating object motion in a 3D environment. You take a model, create keyframes, customize the motion, camera, light, etc. The result is a live video where everything moves like in a real cartoon, commercial or game.

1. Why study animation?

  • To make your project come alive ‚Äî rotation, facial expressions, camera movement
  • Animation ‚Äî the key to gamemade, advertising and visual effects
  • Show you can not only model, but also bring your scenes to life.
  • The ability to make videos, trailers, clips and even short films.

  • Camera animation ‚Äî Camera movement around the subject, zoom in/out.
  • Smoothness of movement ‚Äî working with curves in Graph Editor.
  • Loop animations ‚Äî endless movements (walking, spinning, etc.)
  • Exporting animation to video ‚Äî rendering to .mp4 or .avi.

5. Tips

  • Use the camera for the dynamics of the scene

  • Don't be afraid to experiment - in animation, it's all about creativity
